Dhaka city is the capital of Bangladesh. It is the largest city in the country and serves as its political, economic, and cultural center. The city is known for its vibrant culture, rich history, and diverse population.
Rajshahi is known for its silk industry and is often referred to as the Silk City. It has a rich cultural heritage and is famous for its mangoes, particularly the Langra and Himsagar varieties.
Khulna is the gateway to the Sundarbans, the largest mangrove forest in the world and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The city is known for its shrimp farming and has a rich history as a trading port.
Rangpur is known for its agriculture and is often referred to as the "Land of Agriculture." The city has a rich cultural heritage and is famous for its traditional handicrafts.
